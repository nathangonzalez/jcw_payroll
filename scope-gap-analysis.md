# Scope Gap Analysis - Preventing Missed Scope in Construction Bids  
  
**Research Date:** February 2026  
**Prepared for:** JCW Construction  
**Category:** Estimating Best Practices  
  
---  
  
## Executive Summary  
  
Scope gaps-unforeseen work not included in the original bid-represent one of the most significant profit erosion factors in construction. This research examines common scope gaps, checklist methodologies, software tools, and JCW-specific recommendations to minimize missed scope and protect profit margins.
